# UK-OUI-SPY PRO - Quick Start Guide

Welcome to your new UK-OUI-SPY PRO. Follow these simple steps to get started.

## 1. Charge Your Device

*   Connect the included USB-C cable to the device and a USB power source.
*   The red LED indicates charging. It will turn off when complete.

## 2. Prepare Your SD Card

*   Format a microSD card (up to 32GB) as **FAT32**.
*   Download the latest `oui.csv` file from our GitHub repository.
*   Copy `oui.csv` to the root of the SD card.
*   Insert the card into the device.

## 3. Power On & First Setup

*   Press the power button.
*   The device will start a **Setup Wizard** on its first use.
*   Follow the on-screen instructions by tapping **"NEXT"** and then **"GO"**.

## 4. Basic Operation

*   The device will begin scanning automatically.
*   Use the **navigation bar** at the bottom of the screen to switch between pages (LIST, RADAR, CFG, etc.).
*   On the **LIST** screen, swipe up and down to scroll through detections.

## 5. Connect to the Web Portal

For advanced features, connect your phone or laptop.

1.  **Connect to Wi-Fi**: `OUI-SPY-PRO`
2.  **Password**: `spypro2026`
3.  **Open Browser**: A login page should appear. If not, go to `http://192.168.4.1`.
